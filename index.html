BAR

BAR is the utility token of Ambarcoin, it is the main symbol of our Exchange and it provides the power of a decentralized currency. The Ambarcoin Aggregation Protocol incorporates an algorithm to find the best routes across most liquidity sources.

AMBAR

AMBAR is the governance token, with a total supply of 1,000,000,000. AMBAR was designed to allow the AMBARCOIN community to have their voice heard by voting on proposals through the AMBAR DAO.

The Green Token (BAR Green)

The Green Token (BAR Green) is a token that will be used in the green city. The NFTs will be housed in this city where they can benefit from staying there. Very soon we will be looking at the NFTs and the green city.
